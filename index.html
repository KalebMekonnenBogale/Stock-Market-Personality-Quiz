Stock Market Personality Quiz

Welcome to the Stock Market Personality Quiz - Ethiopian Edition. This program will help you understand your investment style by evaluating your responses. Based on research and psychoanalysis, we assess your risk tolerance and investment preferences

There are 15 questions, and your answers will help determine your risk tolerance.

1. What’s most important to you when you invest?





2. How much risk are you okay with when you invest?





3. How do you feel about market changes and price swings?





4. How long do you want to wait for your investment to grow?





5. If you suddenly got 500,000 Birr, what would you do with it?





6. You hear the government is promoting tech startups. What do you do?





7. A local coffee group is doing well and expanding. Would you invest?





8. A new electric tuk-tuk business is starting in Addis. What do you think?





9. A relative wants you to invest in a family business that’s struggling. What do you do?





10. An international company just launched on the Ethiopian Stock Exchange, but their stock price is unstable. What do you do?





11. A local supermarket chain is planning to expand and might list its stock soon. What would you do?





12. You’ve saved up some money and are ready to invest. What would you do?





13. The Ethiopian government is supporting renewable energy businesses. Would you invest?





14. You’re learning about cryptocurrency in a seminar. How do you feel about it?





15. You get a chance to invest in a tech company modernizing farming. What do you do?





15. Do you prefer to invest in traditional or modern opportunities?
